Features

Everything you need to protect your work

Automatic, silent and thorough — BackupApp remembers every version so you never have to think about saving copies.

Full version history

Every create, edit, rename and delete is recorded automatically in the background.

Browse & restore by date

Filter changes by Today, the last 7 or 30 days, or a specific day — then restore in one click.

Space-smart storage

Identical content is stored once and compressed, so your full history stays surprisingly small.

Office safety net

Recovers Word, Excel and PowerPoint documents even when closed with “Don’t Save” by mistake.

Off-machine backup

Mirror your whole history to a USB drive or a cloud folder like Google Drive — automatically.

Private by design

Everything runs locally on your PC. No account, no cloud lock-in, no tracking — ever.
